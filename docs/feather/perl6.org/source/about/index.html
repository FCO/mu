About perl6.org

perl6.org is, just like like Perl 6, a volunteer driven project. Your contribution is very welcome.

Contributing to perl6.org

The source code for perl6.org is in the pugs repository in docs/feather/perl6.org. Commit bit are handed out freely, please ask on IRC or one of the mailing lists.

The source for individual pages is in the source/ directory, header, footer and menus are in includes/ directory. At least every 15 minutes a cron job runs updates the site from svn and composes it with mowyw.

Feel free to make small additions and changes right away, before larger changes you should discuss these first on #perl6, and look for consensus.

Camelia as the logo for Perl 6 and perl6.org is not negotiable.
