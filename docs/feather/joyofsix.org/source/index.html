[% setvar title The Joy of Six %]

Yeah, yeah, we know. We should already be finished. We should have gotten things done. You're all giddy with anticipation. We are too.

Think about Rakudo * as, well, feature foreplay. Until you get the real deal, you'll dance around it, do some "programming by teasing"; while you can't have the real feature (yet), you'll have to work around it. That's mean, we know.

But it also means you learn a lot more about the language than just getting straight to the proper solution. It'll also teach you patience and searching for just another idea how to solve problem X or Y.

It's also true though that certain things just can't be coded right now, because you can't work around something essential missing.

On the other hand: Maybe getting your feet wet will encourage you to participate by adding the missing essentials?

If you have doubts whether you want to give Six some love - just try it, we basically guarantee new heights of joy and pleasure.

Really talking programming. We promise. Honestly. :)
